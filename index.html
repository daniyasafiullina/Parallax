Welcome to Paris

The City of Love

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most important and influential cities in the world. In terms of tourism, Paris is the second most visited city in Europe after London.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic structures in the world. Built in 1889, it attracts over 7 million visitors each year.

Arc de Triomphe

This monumental arch honors those who fought and died during the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars. It stands proudly on the Champs-Élysées.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces, the Louvre is the world’s most visited museum and a symbol of French culture.
